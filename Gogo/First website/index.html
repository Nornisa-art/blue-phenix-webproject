Baguio City

Baguio City, located in the northern Philippines, is known as the "Summer Capital" due to its cool climate and scenic mountain views. Popular attractions include Burnham Park, Session Road, Mines View Park, Camp John Hay, Baguio Cathedral, and nearby strawberry farms in La Trinidad. The city is a favorite getaway for its refreshing weather, making it a year-round destination for tourists seeking a cool escape from the heat of the lowlands.

Botanical Garden

It's a perfect spot for nature lovers, families, or anyone looking to relax and enjoy the beauty of Baguio's flora and natural surroundings.

Mines View

Mines View Park is an iconic location for both first-time and returning visitors to Baguio, offering a mix of natural beauty, culture, and local charm.

Strawberry Farm

The Strawberry Farms in La Trinidad, near Baguio City, offer visitors the chance to pick fresh strawberries, buy local products, and enjoy scenic views of the surrounding mountains.

Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay in Baguio City is a former American military base turned leisure park, offering nature trails, a golf course, and various recreational activities amid lush pine forests.
